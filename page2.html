Appeal to Devotees

Dear Devotees,


Sri Chennakeshava Temple was re-established on May 2, 1985, through renovation and re-consecration. With a new sanctum and front hall, the temple has served the community for 40 years. However, several essential development works still remain to be completed.

To carry out these works, we have planned a series of initiatives with the kind support of devotees, villagers, and generous donors. The key projects include:

1. Construction of a new building for temple-related activities — 11 cents of land near the temple have been graciously donated for this purpose.
2.Development of essential facilities such as a kitchen, dining hall, assembly hall, and priest quarters.
3.Renovation of the temple well and construction of a water storage tank to ensure sufficient water supply during festivals.
4. Improvement of the temple premises, including tiling and structural enhancements. 

The estimated cost for all these works is ₹1 crore. We humbly request all devotees and well-wishers to join hands and support this noble cause with generosity and devotion.

We sincerely pray to Lord Sri Chennakeshava that every donor, devotee, and their family be blessed with long life, prosperity, peace, and happiness. May they continue to extend their wholehearted support toward the growth and upliftment of the temple.

Managing Committee
Shri Chennakeshava Temple, Nagaramata
